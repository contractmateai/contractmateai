How SignSense uses cookies & local storage

This Cookie Policy explains how SignSense (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies (like localStorage and sessionStorage) on our website. For how we process personal data, see our Privacy Policy.

1) What are cookies & similar tech?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser. We also use localStorage and sessionStorage, which store data in your browser to support site functionality.

2) What we use them for

  • Strictly Necessary (Essential): Core features like keeping the menu state stable or remembering basic preferences. These cannot be turned off in our systems.
  • Functional: Quality-of-life features, e.g., temporarily keeping a draft of your uploaded contract text (in your browser only) so you don’t lose it if you navigate away.
  • Analytics (optional): Privacy-focused analytics (e.g., page views and performance). We do not use them for advertising or profiling.

3) Examples of storage we may use (browser-only)

  • ss_menu_open (sessionStorage): remembers if the mobile menu is open. Expires when you close the tab.
  • ss_upload_draft (localStorage): optional temporary draft of text before analysis to prevent accidental loss. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.
  • ss_consent (localStorage or cookie): your cookie preferences/consent choice so we don’t ask repeatedly.

4) Third-party services

We may use privacy-respecting analytics or infrastructure that set their own cookies. We do not run advertising cookies. See our Privacy Policy for details about data processing with third parties (e.g., AI providers strictly for analysis).

5) Managing your choices

  • Consent banner: On your first visit, you can accept or manage preferences. Essential items are always on because the site won’t work without them.
  • Browser controls: You can block or delete cookies and clear site data (including localStorage/sessionStorage) via your browser settings.
  • Analytics opt-out: If enabled, you can decline analytics in the banner or by clearing consent (Manage Cookies link, if present).

6) Your rights (GDPR/EEA)

If you are in the EEA/UK, essential storage relies on our legitimate interests to provide the service, while optional categories rely on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time using the banner/preferences. For data rights requests, contact support@signsense.io.

7) Cookie/Storage table (illustrative)

  • Category: Essential — Name: ss_menu_open — Type: sessionStorage — Purpose: UI menu state — Duration: session
  • Category: Functional — Name: ss_upload_draft — Type: localStorage — Purpose: preserve draft before analysis — Duration: until cleared
  • Category: Consent — Name: ss_consent — Type: localStorage/cookie — Purpose: remember preferences — Duration: up to 12 months
  • Category: Analytics (optional) — Name: varies by provider — Type: cookie — Purpose: usage metrics — Duration: varies

8) Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (DNT) signal. Because there’s no common industry standard, we don’t respond to DNT at this time. You can still manage cookies with the options above.

9) Updates to this policy

We may update this page as our site evolves. The “Last Updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.

10) Contact

Questions? Email us at support@signsense.io.
