Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Intro

Welcome to SignSense (“we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and AI-powered contract review services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Uploaded Documents: When you upload a contract or document, the content is temporarily processed by our AI systems to generate an analysis. We do not permanently store your documents after processing unless explicitly stated.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable usage information (e.g., page visits, clicks, device type) to improve our services.
  • Email Address (Optional): If you request a downloadable report, we may collect your email. This is optional and never shared or sold.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and analyze uploaded contracts with AI.
  • Deliver requested results or reports back to you.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and security of services.
  • Communicate only if you provide your email for reports or updates.

3. Data Processing & Third Parties

Our AI contract analysis may be powered by OpenAI or similar providers. Documents may be transmitted to these services strictly for analysis purposes. We ensure compliance with GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

4. Data Retention

  • Uploaded contracts are not stored after analysis, except for temporary caching.
  • Emails (if provided) are stored securely and used only to deliver requested reports.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Plausible) to improve site performance. These do not collect contract content.

7. Data Security

We implement safeguards for data during transmission and processing. However, no method is 100% secure; avoid uploading highly sensitive documents.
